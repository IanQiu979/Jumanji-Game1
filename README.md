# Jumanji: The Jaguar’s Eye

A decision-driven adventure game where every choice shapes your fate.

---

## Overview
**Jumanji: The Jaguar’s Eye** is a narrative-based game inspired by the Jumanji universe.  
Players select one of four characters and progress through branching decisions that lead to different outcomes. The goal is simple: survive the game and recover the **Jaguar’s Eye**.

---

## Characters

| Character | Core Strength | Play Style |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| **Dr. Smolder Bravestone** | Strength | Direct, high-risk |
| **Ruby Roundhouse** | Agility | Tactical, fast-paced |
| **Professor Sheldon Oberon** | Intelligence | Analytical, problem-solving |
| **Franklin Finbar** | Support | Resource management |

Each character alters how situations unfold.

---

## Gameplay
- Choose one character at the start  
- Make critical decisions throughout the story  
- Choices affect survival, story paths, and endings  
- Multiple endings encourage replayability  

---

## Objective
Navigate the dangers of Jumanji, make smart decisions, and **find the Jaguar’s Eye** to win.

---

## Features
- Multiple playable characters  
- Branching storylines  
- Choice-based outcomes  
- Replayable design
